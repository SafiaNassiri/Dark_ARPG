# VERGLAS

> *"Evil cannot create anything new — it can only corrupt what the forces of good have already invented."*

A dark isometric ARPG built in Godot 4.6, inspired by **Path of Exile** and **Diablo**.

You are the Rat — a street kid from the Grey Pits whose life of theft and survival is shattered the night your only friend is publicly executed by the Church of the Golden Light. When an inquisitor grabs your arm in the chaos, something inside you erupts. You burn him. You run.

What you are, and what that means for the gods who rule the world above you, is the story of Verglas.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Godot 4.x ([download here](https://godotengine.org/download))

### Running the project
```bash
git clone https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/verglas.git
```
Open Godot 4, click **Import**, and select the `project.godot` file inside the cloned folder.

Hit **F5** or the Play button to run.

## Lore

Verglas is a world ruled by a lie.

The **Church of the Golden Light** teaches that humanity caused the great decay through greed, and that the god **Aurelius** alone holds the world together. In reality, Aurelius murdered the Progenitor — the original creator of the world — and built a religion around his own innocence.

A fragment of the Progenitor's soul was hidden in a human body in the lowest place Aurelius would never look: the gutters.

That fragment is you.

**Key characters:**
- **The Rat** — player character, street kid, Progenitor fragment
- **Vane** — exiled trickster god, companion, been running from Aurelius's lie for two centuries
- **Aurelius** — the golden usurper, final boss, feeds on mortal belief
- **Lyra** — the Mourner, the one who hid you, carries a two-hundred year wound
- **The Goddess Mother** — Old God, dormant, grandmother of the Progenitor, stirring
